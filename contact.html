Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3 months in advance for the best availability and prices. For peak seasons and popular destinations, 6 months advance booking is advised.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we partner with leading insurance providers to offer comprehensive travel coverage. Our packages include medical, cancellation, and luggage protection.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, bookings can be modified or cancelled according to our flexible booking policy. Changes made 30 days before departure are usually free of charge.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 6 or more travelers. Contact our group booking specialist for more information.
